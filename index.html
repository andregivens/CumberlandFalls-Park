Cumberland Falls State Resort Park

Here is the Mapbox map for the Cumberland Falls State Resort Park. *Zoom in to see elevation* Enlarge map

Cumberland Falls State Resort Park is located just southwest of Corbin, Kentucky, and is completely surrounded by the Daniel Boone National Forest. The park covers 1,657 acres (671 ha) and is called after its main attraction, the 68-foot-tall (21-meter) Cumberland Falls.

The goal of this project is to show a cool and interesting place in Kentucky that can be fun to visit.
